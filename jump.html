History
We are a team of talented young blood coming together from good web developing companies in Kerala. Divided between corporate boundaries, we remained networked constantly through our ambitions, ideas and while sharing sharing of expertise during creative leisure moments.

Inspired minds are driving the age according to the young CEO Shahid. signs of his father’s business traits were seen when he tried his hands at his very first project costing him 55,000 INR while he was in a age of 22. From then on he carefully crafted his dream to someday be a great businessman. Each of the V18 team has hands-on-experience over several projects, insight and understandings of the ‘player-customer’ and a burning entrepreneurial desire bringing us to the brink of this soft beginning. Our first client was a travel & tour operator. Our services made a great impact on his business as well as on his customers. Vision18 achieved a small victory and a pat on the back at 3 important places where the client had his offices – Delhi, Chandigarh and Mumbai. We are located in Cochin, South India. We have begun to service clients from Malaysia and the Middle East through our marketing persons in the region.
